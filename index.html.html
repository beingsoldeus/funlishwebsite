About Funlish

Funlish is a dynamic learning center where over 1,000 students from classes V to XII dive into the world of English language and literature. Specializing in ICSE and ISC English, we transform learning into an adventure with fun-filled games, exciting contests, and innovative activities. Join Funlish to ignite your passion for English and excel with confidence!

Why Funlish is Different

At Funlish, students join a global community, collaborating online with peers and top educators from countries like the USA, UK, Russia, Canada, Malaysia, Singapore, Romania, and beyond. Our founder, Mrs. Swati Bakshi, champions the idea that "learning and technology blend beautifully when used wisely." With a heart for education, environmental care, and charity, she leads a vibrant hub where students thrive in English and grow as global citizens. Join Funlish to connect, learn, and shine!
